Davide Staub. Research assistant

Davide Staub completed his degree in Computational Science and Engineering at ETH Zurich. His research has focused on developing and applying scientific machine learning (SciML) techniques, particularly during his time at Oxford and throughout his master's thesis, primarily in the realm of Geophysics and Seismology. Before starting a PhD in SciML, he is interested in exploring how these methodologies, and machine learning in general, can be applied to new fields. Davide has joined the Homan Lab as a Research Assistant, where he contributes to the machine learning aspects of the TRUSTING and DELTA projects.
