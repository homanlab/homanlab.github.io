Alba Lopez. Research intern (alumna)

Alba is doing her master's in psychology at the University of Zurich. She has already gained first clinical experience in the field of autism spectrum disorder and is now doing a research internship in the VELAS project, which focuses on semantic processing in the psychosis spectrum. Alba is particularly interested in neuropsychological research and hopes to deepen her previous knowledge and link it to the broad spectrum of psychotic disorders.
