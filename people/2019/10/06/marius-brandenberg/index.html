Marius Brandenberg. Master's student (alumnus)

Marius is completing his master's in clinical psychology and psychotherapy at the University of Bern. He is particularly interested in psychotic disorders and has completed a clinical internship on the early psychosis unit F2 at the University Hospital of Psychiatry in Zurich. Marius is now working on his master thesis in the VELAS project, together with Noemi as his mentor, where he focuses on cognitive correlates of the schizotypy dimensions.
