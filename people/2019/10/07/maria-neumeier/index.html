Maria Neumeier (alumna)

Maria studied medicine at the University of Erlangen-Nuremberg. During her studies she joined a research project in the field of tumor immunology. She was a resident physician in Psychiatry and Psychotherapy and had a special interest in psychotic disorders. At the lab, she investigated variability of antipsychotic side effects.
