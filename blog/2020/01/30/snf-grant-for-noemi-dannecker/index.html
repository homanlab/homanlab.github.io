SNF grant for Noemi Dannecker

Great news to start the new year: Noemi Dannecker will receive a Doc.CH grant by the Swiss National Science Foundation. This highly competitive grant will support her PhD for the full period of four years. In her project, Noemi is going to apply natural language processing, machine learning, and neuroimaging to identify language markers for mental health and mental illness in the psychosis spectrum. Obviously, we will celebrate this amazing achievement. Congratulations, Noemi!
