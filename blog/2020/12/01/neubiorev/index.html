New publication on brain stimulation

A few months after our paper on individual differences in treatment response to antipsychotics was published, we are happy to share our new paper on treatment effect variability in brain stimulation. Lead by Stephanie and with contributions from Giacomo, Lena, Ellen, and Maria this work evaluated the need for precision medicine in brain stimulation. Interestingly, we found only little evidence for treatment effect variability in brain stimulation, suggesting that the need for personalized or stratified medicine is still an open question. You can find a preprint of this preregistered study here and the code and data on OSF.
