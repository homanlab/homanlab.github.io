Relapse prevention with digital health technology

In our new paper we report results from a US multisite trial that compared digital relapse prevention with usual care. Psychiatric hospitalization is a major driver of cost in the treatment of schizophrenia. Here, we asked whether a technology-enhanced approach to relapse prevention could reduce days spent in a hospital after discharge. The paper was just accepted and can already be read as preprint here, with the code and data on OSF.
