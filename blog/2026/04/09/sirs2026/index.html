Early career awards and poster presentations

Severeal lab members attended this year's SIRS Conference in Florence. This year's meeting again provided an excellent setting for presenting new work and engaging in scientific debate.

The lab contributed four poster presentations, three of which were closely connected to the ERC Synergy project DELTA-LANG. Yves, Akhil, and Xiaoyan presented work from the project, while Finn and Pier presented their research on retinal imaging and network modeling. We are particularly proud of Pier and Finn, who both received an early career award from the Society!
