Retinal clues to mental health

We're happy to share that our new paper is featured on the cover of the May issue of Nature Mental Health!

We found that increased genetic risk for schizophrenia in healthy individuals is associated with subtle but detectable structural changes in the retina. Notably, genes involved in neuroinflammation were particularly linked to retinal thinning.

Our findings help to provide confidence that the genetic factors and neuroinflammatory mechanisms which underlie schizophrenia affect not just the brain but also the retina.
