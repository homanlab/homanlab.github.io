Members of the lab attended the 20th anniversary edition of the [[https://www.unige.ch/ABIM/][Alpine
Brain Imaging Meeting]] in Champéry, a long running conference known for
its interdisciplinary exchange in cognitive and clinical
neuroscience. This year's meeting again provided an excellent setting
for presenting new work and engaging in scientific debate.

The lab contributed four poster presentations, three of which were
closely connected to the [[https://erc.europa.eu/news-events/news/synergy-grants-2023-examples-projects][ERC Synergy project DELTA-LANG]]. [[https://homanlab.github.io/people/2019/10/13/finn-rabe-phd/][Finn]],
[[https://homanlab.github.io/people/2019/10/11/akhil-misra/][Akhil]], and [[https://homanlab.github.io/people/2019/10/13/xiaoyan-wu/][Xiaoyan]] presented work from the project,
while Marlene presented her research on circadian disruption in
bipolar disorder. We are particularly proud of [[https://homanlab.github.io/people/2019/10/13/xiaoyan-wu/][Xiaoyan]], whose
poster was awarded the Best Poster Award.
