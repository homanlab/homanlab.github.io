New preprint on antidepressants

A few months after our paper on individual differences in treatment response to antipsychotics was published, we are excited to announce that we just posted a follow up study to PsyArxiv. Klaus Munkholm, MD, DMSc, from the Nordic Cochrane Centre led this project that assessed a potential personal element of treatment response in antidepressants. You can find the preprint here and the code and data on OSF.
