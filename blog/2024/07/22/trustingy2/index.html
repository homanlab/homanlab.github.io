EU Horizon project TRUSTING: Annual Meeting in Leiden

Wrapping up a busy first year, the TRUSTING consortium met for its second annual meeting in the last week of June, this time in Leiden in the Netherlands. In TRUSTING, we aim to apply quantitative language analysis to prevent relapse in psychotic disorders. We had an inspiring and eventful week at the Lorentz Center and even spent an evening at the beach.
