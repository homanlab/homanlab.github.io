Lab Retreat

We have been growing recently as a lab and thought it would be helpful to spend a whole day together for teambuilding and scientific exchange. Perfectly organized by Roya, Nils Lang, Giacomo, and of course Karolina, the Homan Lab thus gathered for a retreat at UZH on May 23. One of the highlights was a scientific speed dating where lab members took 6 minutes to pitch their ideas to one another. It was also a great opportunity to welcome our latest members Lucia (who started in May) and Yves (who will start in June).

While it rained during the retreat, skies cleared up in the evening and we wrapped up the day nicely at Kleine Freiheit.
