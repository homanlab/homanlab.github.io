Severeal lab members attended this year's [[https://sirs.societyconference.com/conf/#sessions/conf10021][SIRS Conference]] in
Florence. This year's meeting again provided an excellent setting for
presenting new work and engaging in scientific debate.

The lab contributed four poster presentations, three of which were
closely connected to the [[https://erc.europa.eu/news-events/news/synergy-grants-2023-examples-projects][ERC Synergy project DELTA-LANG]]. [[https://homanlab.github.io/people/2019/10/11/yves-pauli/][Yves]], [[https://homanlab.github.io/people/2019/10/11/akhil-misra/][Akhil]],
and [[https://homanlab.github.io/people/2019/10/13/xiaoyan-wu/][Xiaoyan]] presented work from the project, while [[https://homanlab.github.io/people/2019/10/13/finn-rabe-phd/][Finn]] and [[https://homanlab.github.io/people/2019/10/08/pierfrancesco-sarti/][Pier]]
presented their research on retinal imaging and network modeling. We
are particularly proud of Pier and Finn, who both received an early
career award from the Society!
